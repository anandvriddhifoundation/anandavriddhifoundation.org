## 2. About Section

## 4. Three Pillars of Compassion
- **Subtitle:** What We Do
- **Heading:** Three Pillars of Compassion
- **Description:** Our social outreach spans three vital segments, each united by one goal — spreading joy and knowledge through immersive virtual experiences.
- **Segments:**
  1. **Old Age Homes** | *Elderly Care & Well-Being*
     - We bring immersive VR travel experiences to senior citizens, helping combat loneliness and boredom. From the comfort of their homes, elders virtually visit the Taj Mahal, Kerala backwaters, and Himalayan peaks — sparking joy and supporting cognitive health.
  2. **Orphanages** | *Dreams Without Boundaries*
     - Children in orphanages rarely get the chance to travel. Through VR, we give them the gift of exploration — letting them discover India's heritage, wildlife, and wonders. Every session brings wonder, laughter, and new dreams.
  3. **Schools** | *Education for All Abilities*
     - We partner with schools serving economically weaker sections and differently-abled students. VR transforms learning from textbooks into experiences — making geography, history, and culture come alive in ways students never imagined.

## 5. Dekho Apna Desh Programme
- **Subtitle:** Our Signature Programme
- **Heading:** 🇮🇳 Dekho Apna Desh
- **Description:** In partnership with **TellMe Digiinfotech**, we procure high-quality VR footage of India's most iconic destinations and bring them to communities who may never get to travel. A virtual pilgrimage of joy, wonder, and national pride.
- **Key Features:**
  1. **Immersive VR Tours:** 360° high-quality footage of India's heritage sites, temples, hill stations, and national landmarks.
  2. **Pan-India Coverage:** From the Himalayas to Kanyakumari, from Rajasthan forts to Kerala backwaters — India comes alive.
  3. **Joy & Education:** Every session combines entertainment with learning — geography, culture, and history through experience.
